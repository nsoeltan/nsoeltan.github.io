About Me

Nafisa Soeltan works as an IT Consultant/Business Analyst within the Life Sciences Industry.

She has an MBA in Management Information Systems and a BA in Psychology from Rowan University in New Jersey. She also has a BS Certificate in Business Economics from Inholland University in the Netherlands.

Currently, she is taking coding classes as part of Penn's LPS 2018 Coding Bootcamp with the objective of not only challenging her creativity but to also increase her technical knowledge of information systems.

She loves traveling around the world and exploring new cultures. Her most exciting experience during her travels has been riding a smelly camel on a beach and scuba diving in Egypt. The most breathtaking beaches she has been to are in Greece.
